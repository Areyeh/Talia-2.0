Product Description

Summer Coast is a vibrant and colorful artwork that captures the essence of a perfect summer day by the sea. The painting features a coastal scene with warm sand, blue waters, and playful details that evoke a sense of nostalgia and joy.

This artwork is part of Talia's Summer Collection, which celebrates the simple pleasures of the season through vivid colors and whimsical imagery. Each piece in this collection is hand-painted using acrylic and pencils on high-quality paper.

The original artwork measures 29.5×21 cm and comes unframed, allowing you to choose a frame that complements your space. Prints are also available in A4 and A3 sizes, printed on premium archival paper to ensure longevity and color accuracy.
